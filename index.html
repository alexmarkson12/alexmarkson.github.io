How to play?

It's super simple. The "Judge" picks up a black prompt card. The player sitting clockwise to the "Judge", the "Pitcher", picks up a red card. Red cards contain the subject. The Pitcher has 1 minute to think, and 2 minutes to pitch. Once finished, the next person becomes the pitcher until everyone has gone. The Judge then chooses who had the best pitch, and gives them the black card. Who ever has the most black cards win!
